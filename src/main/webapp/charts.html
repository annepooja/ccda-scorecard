Section Level Scores Summary

The Section level heat map below Once scoring is complete, a section level heat map will appear below. The heat map displays a top level view of each of the sections that were scored, their grades, and the number of issues per section that need to be addressed. You can view additional details of the issues such as line numbers in the XML causing the issue, Links to HL7 examples, and C-CDA IG references by selecting the specific section of interest.
