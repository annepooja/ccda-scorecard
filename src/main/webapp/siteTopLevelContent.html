C-CDA R2.1 Scorecard

Information about the SITE C-CDA R2.1 Scorecard

The SITE C-CDA R2.1 Scorecard provides an enhanced level of interoperability for C-CDA documents by using a comprehensive scoring system, which allows implementers to improve the data quality and representation of their C-CDA documents, thereby increasing the precision of documents used in the real-world. The C-CDA R2.1 Scorecard scores a C-CDA document against a specified set of criteria and provides feedback on potential improvements. The SITE C-CDA R2.1 Scorecard goes above and beyond the 2015 S&CC requirements by providing feedback based on the content of the provided C-CDA document.

 

C-CDA Validator and C-CDA Scorecard Relationship

The C-CDA Validator validates the conformance of C-CDA documents to the respective HL7 C-CDA Implementation Guide as well as the ONC 2014 and 2015 Certification Editions' regulations and objectives. The SITE C-CDA Scorecard compliments the validator testing by providing an enhanced level of non-standard based criteria deemed important for interoperability.
