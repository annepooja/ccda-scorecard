C-CDA R2.1 Scorecard

(Alpha Release)

The C-CDA Scorecard based on the early work done by the SMART team, promotes best practices in C-CDA implementation by assessing key aspects of the structured data found in individual documents. It's a tool to help implementers gain visibility into how well and how often best practices are followed — and also to summarize progress with a rough quantitative assessment, highlighting improvements that can be made today. These best practices and quantitative scoring criteria have been developed by HL7 through the HL7-ONC Cooperative agreement to improve standards.
