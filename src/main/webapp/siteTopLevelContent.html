Information about the SITE C-CDA R2.1 Validator

The goal of the SITE C-CDA R2.1 Validator is to validate conformance of C-CDA documents to the standard in order to promote interoperability. It is a resource to validate the conformance of C-CDA documents to ONC 2014 and 2015 Certification Editions regulations and objectives, or the base HL7 C-CDA Implementation Guide.
